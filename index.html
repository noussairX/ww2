INTERNET.

The Internet is a global network that allows people to communicate and share information and resources via computers and smartphones. The Internet has many benefits, the most important of which is access to information, as it allows people to search for many and diverse information, social communication, as it enables community members to communicate with their friends and family through social media. In addition, distance learning in the field of study and professional development. The Internet can be used to do some daily activities, such as booking appointments, shopping by searching for products and buying through online stores, as well as watching movies, programs and news using streaming platforms. These are some benefits, there are also some negative effects of the Internet on society, including social isolation, where an excessive focus on the Internet may lead to isolation and reduced communication with people, which negatively affects mental health. Constant preoccupation with the Internet can lead to distraction and reduced productivity. Also, the use of electronic devices before bed may affect the quality of sleep due to light radiation. The most important thing is that there may be security and privacy risks, such as hacking accounts and stealing personal information, as well as the abundance of information can lead to the spread of false news. As people have become addicted to the Internet and from different sides, some are addicted to games that are designed to be addicted to them, and these games can be destructive to one's psyche, especially children and adolescents.
